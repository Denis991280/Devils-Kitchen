Spicy Szechuan Fish

Spicy Szechuan Fish is an electrifying culinary adventure that tingles the taste buds with its bold and fiery flavors. This dish features tender fish fillets bathed in a spicy, aromatic sauce infused with Szechuan peppercorns and dried red chili peppers, creating a symphony of heat and numbing sensations. The umami-rich doubanjiang (Szechuan chili bean paste) adds depth to the sauce, while the marriage of garlic, ginger, and green onions elevates its complexity. Each bite of Spicy Szechuan Fish is a delightful rollercoaster of flavor, striking the perfect balance between intense spiciness and irresistible savory notes, leaving a memorable and addictive impression.

2x Fish
